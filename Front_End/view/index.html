    We offer expert care across multiple veterinary specialties to ensure your pet receives the best possible treatment for their unique needs.

    • Cardiology
    • Neurology
    • Internal Medicine
    • Puppy & Kitten Care

    Cardiology

    Heart health is vital for your pet's longevity and quality of life.

    Our cardiology team specializes in diagnosing and treating heart conditions in pets, including congenital defects, heart murmurs, and congestive heart failure. We use advanced diagnostics like echocardiograms and ECGs to provide precise care tailored to your pet's needs.

    Neurology

    Expert care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders.

    Our neurology department treats conditions like seizures, spinal injuries, and degenerative diseases. Using MRI and CT scans, we accurately diagnose issues affecting your pet's nervous system and develop customized treatment plans, including surgery if needed.

    Internal Medicine

    Comprehensive care for complex medical conditions.

    Our internal medicine specialists handle chronic illnesses like diabetes, kidney disease, and gastrointestinal disorders. We perform advanced diagnostics, including ultrasounds and endoscopies, to provide accurate diagnoses and effective long-term management.

    Puppy & Kitten Care

    Specialized care for your newest family members.

    We provide complete wellness plans for young pets, including vaccinations, deworming, nutrition counseling, and early behavior training. Our team ensures your puppy or kitten gets the best start for a healthy, happy life.

    Ophthalmology

    Protecting your pet's vision and eye health.

    Our ophthalmology team diagnoses and treats eye conditions including cataracts, glaucoma, and corneal ulcers. We perform specialized surgeries when needed and provide ongoing care to maintain your pet's eyesight.

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Get answers to common questions about our pet care services and veterinary practice

    What are your clinic hours?

    Our clinic is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and major holidays. Emergency services are available 24/7 through our on-call veterinarian.

    Do I need to make an appointment?

    Yes, we recommend scheduling appointments to ensure we can dedicate proper time to your pet. However, we do accept walk-ins for urgent cases, with priority given to emergencies. You can book appointments online or by phone.

    What vaccinations does my pet need?

    Core vaccinations for dogs include rabies, distemper, parvovirus, and adenovirus. For cats, we recommend rabies, feline distemper, calicivirus, and herpesvirus. Additional vaccines may be recommended based on your pet's lifestyle and risk factors. We'll create a personalized vaccination plan during your visit.

    How often should my pet have a check-up?

    Healthy adult pets should have annual wellness exams. Senior pets (7+ years) or pets with chronic conditions may need biannual visits. Puppies and kittens require more frequent check-ups during their first year for vaccinations and growth monitoring.

    Do you offer emergency services?

    Yes, we provide emergency care during regular hours. After hours, our on-call veterinarian is available for true emergencies. For life-threatening situations when we're unavailable, we recommend the Animal Emergency Hospital located at 123 Veterinary Way.

    What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, and pet insurance claims. Payment is required at time of service. We can provide estimates for procedures and offer CareCredit financing options for qualifying clients.

    How can I prepare my pet for their visit?

    Bring any medical records, a list of medications, and a stool sample if requested. Keep dogs on leash and cats in carriers. For anxious pets, consider bringing treats or a favorite toy. Avoid feeding 2-3 hours before appointments that might involve sedation.

    Do you offer boarding services?

    Yes, we provide medical boarding for pets with special needs who require professional supervision. All boarding pets must be current on vaccinations and free of contagious conditions. Space is limited, so we recommend making reservations well in advance.
